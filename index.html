About the Department

SECE aspires to provide its students the best in their life. In this respect, the college houses a well-qualified and highly experienced Placement and Training Team, under the capable administration of the placement director, which aims at providing placement and training services to the students. The dynamic placement cell serves as the foundation for many careers by guiding students in the right direction through continuous training, counseling and arranging campus recruitments .This cell creates a meticulous database and assists the students in getting placed in national & multinational companies through campus recruitments.

Training & Development Programme

Sri Eshwar College of Engineering is an institution that has impeccable belief and trust in the effectiveness of training programmes which has the potential to influence and enhance the Knowledge, Skills, Attitude and Behaviour of the individuals.
